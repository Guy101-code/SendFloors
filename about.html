Our Journey

At SendFloors, we're passionate about providing top-notch flooring solutions to our customers. Our journey began in 2021, with a vision to revolutionize the flooring industry by offering premium products, exceptional customer service, and unparalleled expertise.

Our team of experienced professionals has years of combined experience in the flooring industry, and we're dedicated to helping our customers find the perfect flooring solution for their needs. Whether you're a homeowner, contractor, or business owner, we're committed to providing you with the best possible experience.

Our mission is to build long-lasting relationships with our customers, and to provide them with the highest quality flooring products and services. We believe in going above and beyond to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their flooring experience.

At SendFloors, we're not just about helping you renovate your home - we're about helping our customers create beautiful, functional, and safe spaces that they'll love for years to come. We're proud to be a part of our customers' journeys, and we look forward to serving you.

Once you select your flooring, we provide a detailed quotation, and upon agreement, we prepare the area and install the flooring according to industry standards. Finally, we conduct a thorough inspection to ensure quality and provide guidance on maintenance for your new flooring.
